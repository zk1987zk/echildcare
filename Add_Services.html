Add your service to the Queensland Childcare Index

Please note that virtual businesses do not qualify for listing in this directory, as we are a location-based directory, allowing people to find local services

To be listed in this directory you must meet at least one of the following criteria:

  • Have a listing on the mychild directory
  • Have a listing on the ACECQA directory
  • Have a business name currently registered with ASIC
  • Have a website with a domain name you are paying for
  • Have a White Pages entry for one of the displayed phone numbers, matching this address
