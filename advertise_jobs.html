Required fields are marked with a *

No HTML may be entered here - if you would like to format your entry, please use Textile formatting [More info...].
Quick summary: leave a gap between paragraphs and lines that start with a star then a space will be a bulleted list.

Briefly tell the jobseeker about who they will be working for.

Short example: "Smittie's Centre is a 39 place, award winning long day care centre that offers the best in early childhood education for children aged 2-6 years."


Tell the jobseeker what you are looking for.

Short example: "We are looking for a university qualified Early Childhood Teacher with at least 3 years experience."

Be expansive and tell them exactly what you are looking for - the more detailed you are, the better quality your applicants will be.


Tell the jobseeker how to apply.

Short example: "Please phone 1111 1111 and ask for our Director, Lorraine."
